Park and Open Space Management


In the previous view we could see many of the same spaces as here, but not who managed those spaces.

Here you can see that Green spaces are federally managed(BLM,ODFW,etc), Red spaces area managed by the county, and Blue again by the city of Corvallis.

Also you can see there is an orange area. This outlines the Mcdunn forest area that is managed by Oregonstate University

City Parks Playgrounds
